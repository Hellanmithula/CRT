Delicious Chicken Biriyani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken (cleaned)
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup Curd/Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices (Bay leaf, Cloves, Cinnamon, Cardamom)
  • Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala
  • Coriander & Mint Leaves
  • Salt & Oil as needed

Steps to Make Biriyani:

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and cook until 70% done.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add whole spices, ginger-garlic paste, and sauté well.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add chicken, spices, salt, and cook until chicken is 80% cooked.
  6. Mix in curd and chopped mint & coriander leaves.
  7. Layer the chicken masala and half-cooked rice in a pot.
  8. Cover and cook (dum) on low heat for 15–20 minutes.
  9. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  10. Serve hot with raita or boiled egg!
